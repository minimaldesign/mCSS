## How to use tokens

1. Override low level tokens in your theme.
1. Customize abstracted tokens for `elements` files in your theme.
1. Customize abstracted tokens for `layout` files in your theme.
1. Use low level tokens directly in your `component` files.

## Low level tokens

### Typography

There are 3 font stacks set up by default: `text`, `display`, and `monospace`.

The stacks are optimized for fonts available on device, to maximize for speed and alleviate any layout shifts or flashes.

You can find more stacks at [Modern Font Stacks](https://modernfontstacks.com).

If you'd like to bring in your own fonts or [Google fonts](https://fonts.google.com), you can override the `display` and `text` tokens inside your theme file.

**Note:** in case you're curious, `ui-sans-serif` is the system default, while `sans-serif` is the browser default, and `system-ui` is the system default, wether serif or sans-serif.
